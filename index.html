Who are we?

The Western Engineering Mars Rover team is a student-led club driven towards the goal of building a rover that is capable of accomplishing various tasks in environments similar to ones found on the surface of mars. Our team is comprised of various students from multiple disciplines. As an organization, we strive to inspire our members and members of the public to become more involved in science and technology through robotics

Our Goal

Our goal as a team is to compete in and win the Canadian International Rover Challenge.
