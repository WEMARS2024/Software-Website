Mechanical

The WE Mars mechanical team is responsible for the design, testing, and assembly of all the components that make up our rover. From material selection and CAD to prototyping and manufacturing, we ensure that every part of the rover meets our standards. Our team focuses on creating innovative, reliable, and efficient designs to tackle any challenge our rover might face, whether itâ€™s navigating rough terrain or performing delicate tasks with a robotic arm. Collaboration is key to our success, as we work closely with the electrical and software teams to integrate our mechanical systems. Currently, we are working on a new rover, aiming to surpass our previous rovers capabilities with our gained knowledge from past competitions.
